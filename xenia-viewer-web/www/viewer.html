Viewer MVP

Synthetic "remote screen" rendered from sealed FRAME envelopes. Mouse and keyboard events flow back as sealed Input. Loopback: sender and receiver are the same browser tab. Everything WASM, no server.

Real cross-machine remote desktop needs a server-side screen capture, a transport (WebSocket or WebTransport), and real input injection. That's Track-B engineering. This page demonstrates the wire works under realistic-shaped traffic: 160×120 RGBA frames at ~15 fps + keyboard/mouse events on the reverse path.
