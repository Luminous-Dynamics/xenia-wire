Consent ceremony walk-through

Interactive demo of Xenia's consent layer, running entirely in your browser. Everything is local — no server, no network, no real session.

Each side (technician and end-user) has its own Ed25519 device key. Messages are signed by the sender's key and the signature covers the bincode-encoded message body, not the sealed envelope — so consent can be verified independently of the AEAD session.
